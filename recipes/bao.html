Custard Bao Buns

Fresh, pillowy soft, and delicious!

Nothing hits the spot like a warm, fluffy, not-too-sweet steamed bun! These custard bao buns are easy to make and are perfect as a snack or quick breakfast! You can also shape the dough into cute animals, adding a personal touch!

Ingredients (makes 8 buns)

  • 200g + 160g milk
  • 20g butter
  • 2 egg yolks
  • vanilla extract (optional)
  • 25g + 10g white sugar
  • 20g tapioca starch
  • 260g all-purpose flour
  • 2g salt
  • 3g instant yeast
  • 4g baking powder
  • 10g vegetable oil
