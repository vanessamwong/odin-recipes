Microwaved Sweet Potato

Piping hot and ready to go!

Delicious and chock full of vitamins, a microwaved sweet potato makes for an easy and filling snack! Try it with peanut butter for extra deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • sweet potato

Steps

  1. Wash with water and peel the skin off if you wish. Pierce it with a knife or fork to create holes for ventilation.
  2. Wrap with a damp paper towel to create steam.
  3. Microwave in 3 minute intervals, flipping in between, until tender. This can be tested by poking the sweet potato with a utensil.
  4. Allow to cool for at least 5 minutes before digging in.
